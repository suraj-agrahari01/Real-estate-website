STUNNING YANKEE POINT VIEWS

456 Oak Street

On the edge of the Pacific with panoramic views across Yankee Point and beyond to the endless horizon, this stunning 4 bedroom, 6,400+ square foot, ultra-luxury home offers scenes of breaching whales, playful otters and stunning sunsets. Designed as a stand alone piece of artwork unto itself, 34 Yankee Point is truly one of the most stunning homes on the entire California coast. Custom one-of-a-kind details throughout the home make this property a magnificent sculpture of comfort, quality, and unparalleled oceanfront living. Highlights include an ocean-view spa and terrace, a sunken living room with soaring floor-to-ceiling windows, three fireplaces, two offices, and a delightful family room/entertainment space with a game room and an expansive built-in saltwater aquarium. Thoughtful design, exacting execution, world-class location, and a graceful melding of steel, stone, copper and glass with an unbelievable dedication to detail all combine to create one of the most jaw-dropping oceanfront homes available.
