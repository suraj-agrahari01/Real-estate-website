CONTEMPORARY TURN-KEY ESTATE IN TEHAMA

789 Elm Street

Located amongst the rolling hillsides and cascading pines of Clint Eastwood’s world-renowned Tehama Golf Club, this turnkey, 6-acre, gated estate in Carmel Valley features stunning contemporary design and an impressive list of amenities. The expansive stone-paved motor court connects the impressive main house and spacious guest house with an alluring fountain decorating the center. The open floor plan flows effortlessly through the grand foyer into the living room, dining room, kitchen, and through to the backyard and pool areas. The gourmet kitchen features an illuminated Onyx countertop set to dazzle guests and includes a large butler’s pantry. Other highlights include an outdoor kitchen equipped with a barbeque and custom-built pizza oven, sauna, and an oversized heated pool with fountains, fire pits, and colorful lighting. The upper deck overlooks the pool area and enjoys picturesque views of the Santa Lucia mountains. The estate offers a stunning and private setting to entertain family and friends, with almost 10,000 square feet of living space and the 2-bedroom guest house affords a separate retreat with full kitchen and bath. Car enthusiasts will also enjoy the combined 9-plus car garage space. Truly a modern masterpiece, this is where sunny and serene Carmel Valley meets elegant, luxurious living. Property is available fully furnished, with the exception of some art, interior design by Hulburd Design.
